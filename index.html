Tournament Location

This tournament will be held at the Tulsa Rugby Club pitch — located in the Heart of the River Parks, directly south of the Gathering Place. It is located at 3772-3798 Riverside Dr, Tulsa, OK.

Parking

  • Use 31st Street parking, 41st Street parking, and nearby street parking where available.
  • Please consolidate vehicles where possible and follow posted signs.
  • No parking will be allowed on the field except emergency access and event operations.

Tulsa Rugby Club

Founded in 1974, Tulsa Rugby Club has a proud tradition of competitive rugby and community involvement in Oklahoma. Home to both men's and women's teams, the club competes in regional and national competitions. Tulsa Rugby is honored to host the Parrothead Rugby 7s tournament and showcase the best of rugby in the heartland.
