Tournament Details

The men’s tournament will feature six teams split into two pools of three. Each team will play two pool matches, followed by a knockout round where all teams are guaranteed at least one more game. The top teams will advance to the semifinals and finals, while remaining teams will compete in placement matches, including a Plate Final.

The women’s tournament will include four teams in a single pool, playing round-robin style. The top two teams will advance to a final match, while the remaining teams will compete for third place.

Registration Process

Tulsa Rugby Club

Founded in 1974, Tulsa Rugby Club has a proud tradition of competitive rugby and community involvement in Oklahoma. Home to both men's and women's teams, the club competes in regional and national competitions. Tulsa Rugby is honored to host the Parrothead Rugby 7s tournament and showcase the best of rugby in the heartland.
